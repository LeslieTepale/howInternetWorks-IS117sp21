Packets, Routing & Reliability





When making connections between servers and a computer, the internet travels through different directions rather than having a di rect connection to the server. Doing so can mitigate issues when multiple computers use the same service at once. It’s important that information doesn’t follow a fixed path because of this, at times it even takes different routes to retrieve information. T his information is called a packet of information. There are limits to what kind of information can be sent over, however, as at times the information has to be broken into smaller packets for it to reach the server.
Routers allow packets to be transported smoothly to arrive at the destination, perhaps at different times or out of order. The routes transport the information in a more cost and time efficient manner. This is what allows the internet to be reliable.
